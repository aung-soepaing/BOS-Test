About This Page

This diagnostics page shows your current authentication session information for local and Entra sign-ins. It's useful for debugging SSO issues before they cause access denial errors.

Tip: If assignment changes were made in Entra, try logging out and logging in again with a fresh token. Sometimes changes in Entra assignments take a few minutes to propagate.
