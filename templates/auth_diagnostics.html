About This Page

This diagnostics page shows your current authentication session information and what roles your Entra token contains. It's useful for debugging SSO issues before they cause access denial errors.

Tip: If you see "No roles found in token" but expect roles, try logging out and logging in again with a fresh token. Sometimes changes in Entra assignments take a few minutes to propagate.
